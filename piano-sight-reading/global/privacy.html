Privacy Policy (English)

1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Piano Sight Reading: Notes" (also known as 钢琴识谱练习; the "App"), provided by Lumen Labs ("we", "our", or "us"). It explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account and we do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number directly through the App.

Information stored only on your device (local data)

The App stores data locally on your device using local storage technologies (such as Hive / hive_flutter), including practice records, progress, level, achievements, settings, and notification schedules (e.g. for practice reminders). This data is not sent to our servers and is not backed up by us. If you uninstall the App or clear app data, this information cannot be recovered.

Information that may be collected by third-party services

The App integrates third-party advertising and analytics services. These services may automatically collect information such as:

  • Device information (e.g. device model, operating system version, language, Android SDK version for permission and compatibility logic);
  • Usage data (e.g. screens or features you use, session duration);
  • Approximate location (e.g. coarse location inferred from IP);
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID);
  • Crash logs and performance data.

We do not control the data collected by these third parties. Their practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for purposes such as:

  • Improving the stability, performance, and features of the App;
  • Understanding how users interact with the App and optimizing the learning and practice experience;
  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services;
  • Showing relevant advertisements through our advertising partners;
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues and misuse;
  • Using device information where necessary for permission handling (e.g. notification, Bluetooth for MIDI) and compatibility.

4. Third-Party Services and SDKs

The App uses the following types of third-party services and libraries, which may collect or process data as described in their own privacy policies. For more details on each provider’s data practices, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

  • Advertising: Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) and other third-party ad networks (e.g. through our overseas ad SDK, ads_sdk_overseas). These may collect device information, advertising identifiers, and coarse location to deliver and measure ads. Privacy Policy (Google/AdMob): https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics services such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or other partners (e.g. Umeng where applicable) to understand usage and improve the App. These services may collect device and usage data. Privacy Policy (Umeng): https://www.umeng.com/policy. For Google analytics services, see also https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Local storage: Hive / hive_flutter — used to store practice records, progress, level, achievements, and settings locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Device and permissions: device_info_plus (to obtain device and system information for permission and compatibility logic), permission_handler (to request permissions such as notifications and Bluetooth for features like practice reminders and external MIDI devices).
  • Local notifications: flutter_local_notifications, timezone, flutter_timezone — used only for local practice reminders. Notification schedules are stored on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Network and external content: If the App sends data to our servers or third parties (e.g. via dio), we use it for the purposes described in this policy. When you open external links or in-app web pages (url_launcher, flutter_inappwebview), the privacy practices of those third-party sites apply; we do not control their data collection.
  • Other functionality: package_info_plus (app version and related information for support and compatibility). The App also uses libraries for audio and MIDI functionality (e.g. flutter_midi_pro, flutter_pitch_detection, flutter_midi_command). Audio and MIDI processing is performed locally on your device; we do not upload audio content.

We recommend that you review the privacy policies of Google (including AdMob and Google Analytics), and any other providers we link to, for details on their data practices.

5. Permissions We Request

The App may request the following device permissions. We only request permissions necessary for the features described below:

  • INTERNET
    Purpose: Required for loading and displaying in-app advertisements, sending anonymous usage statistics to third-party analytics services, and downloading any online content.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS, SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM, RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
    Purpose: Used to schedule and deliver local practice reminder notifications at the time you set. RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED is used to reschedule your reminders after a device reboot so they are not lost. We do not use these permissions to send push messages from our servers.
  • VIBRATE
    Purpose: Used to provide optional haptic feedback (vibration) during in-app interactions where you have enabled this setting.
  • RECORD_AUDIO (Microphone)
    Purpose: Used exclusively for the "piano audio recognition" feature, which listens to the sound of a real acoustic or digital piano through your device microphone to identify the notes you are playing in real time. Audio is processed locally on your device and is NOT recorded, stored, or uploaded to any server.
  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT, BLUETOOTH_SCAN, ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION (Android 11 and below)
    Purpose: Used to discover and connect to external MIDI devices (e.g. MIDI keyboards or controllers) via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION is required on Android 11 and below because the operating system requires it for BLE scanning; the App does not use your location for any other purpose. On Android 12 and above, we use BLUETOOTH_SCAN with the neverForLocation flag so that your location is not accessed.

We do not use any of the above permissions for purposes other than those described here. You can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings; revoking a permission will disable the related feature.

6. Data Storage and Security

Data collected through third-party SDKs may be processed and stored on those providers’ servers. We take reasonable measures to work with providers that apply appropriate security practices. Local data (e.g. practice records, settings) remains on your device and is not transmitted to us for storage.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially reasonable measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is primarily intended for general users and music learners and is not specifically designed for children under 13. The content is suitable for learning; children under 13 may use the App with the knowledge and consent of a parent or legal guardian.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it as soon as reasonably possible. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with such information, please contact us.

8. Your Choices

  • Advertising: You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your device settings.
  • Local data: You can clear app data or uninstall the App; all local progress, records, and settings will be lost and we cannot restore them.
  • Uninstalling: Uninstalling the App will stop further collection of data by the App; third-party SDKs may have already collected data as described in their policies.

9. International Use

Information collected by third-party services may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the App, you consent to such transfer and processing, which may be subject to different data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Developer: Lumen Labs
  • Contact: support@lumen-labs.app

隐私政策（中文版）

一、简介与适用范围

本隐私政策适用于由 Lumen Labs（下称“我们”）提供的移动应用程序 《钢琴识谱练习》（英文名 Piano Sight Reading: Notes；下称“本应用”），用于说明我们在您使用本应用时如何收集、使用和保护相关信息。

二、我们收集的信息

我们不会要求您在应用内注册账户，也不会通过本应用主动收集您的姓名、邮箱、电话等可直接识别您个人身份的信息。

仅存储在您设备上的信息（本地数据）

本应用使用本地存储技术（如 Hive / hive_flutter）在您设备本地保存数据，包括练习记录、进度、等级、成就、设置以及通知日程（例如练习提醒）。这些数据不会上传到我们的服务器，我们也不对其进行备份。若您卸载本应用或清除应用数据，上述信息将无法恢复。

可能由第三方服务收集的信息

本应用集成了第三方广告与统计服务。这些服务可能会自动收集如下类型的信息：

  • 设备信息（如设备型号、操作系统版本、语言、Android SDK 版本等，用于权限与兼容性逻辑）；
  • 使用数据（如您使用的页面或功能、使用时长）；
  • 大致位置信息（如基于 IP 推断的粗略位置）；
  • 广告标识符（如 Google 广告 ID）；
  • 崩溃日志与性能数据。

我们无法控制上述第三方所收集的数据，其行为受其自身隐私政策约束。

三、我们如何使用这些信息

我们可能将上述信息用于以下用途：

  • 改进本应用的稳定性、性能与功能；
  • 了解用户如何使用本应用，并据此优化学习与练习体验；

  • 发现、预防并处理技术问题与滥用行为；
  • 在必要时使用设备信息处理权限（如通知、蓝牙连接 MIDI）及兼容性。

四、第三方服务与 SDK

本应用使用以下类型的第三方服务与库，其可能按各自隐私政策收集或处理数据。 关于各提供方的数据处理方式，请参阅其隐私政策。

  • 广告：Google Mobile Ads（AdMob） 及其他第三方广告网络（例如通过我们使用的海外广告 SDK：ads_sdk_overseas）。这些服务可能收集设备信息、广告标识符、大致位置等用于投放与衡量广告。隐私政策（Google/AdMob）：https://policies.google.com/privacy。

建议您查阅 Google（含 AdMob、Google Analytics）及我们提供的其他服务提供方的隐私政策，以了解其数据处理方式。

五、我们申请的权限

本应用可能会申请以下设备权限。我们仅申请实现下述功能所必要的权限：

  • INTERNET（网络访问）
    用途：用于在应用内加载和展示广告，以及向第三方统计服务发送匿名使用数据。
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS / SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM / RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED（通知与定时）
    用途：用于在您设定的时间发送本地练习提醒通知。RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED 用于在设备重启后自动恢复您的提醒计划，确保提醒不因重启而丢失。我们不会通过这些权限向您推送服务器消息。
  • VIBRATE（振动）
    用途：在您开启振动反馈设置时，提供可选的操作振动反馈。
  • RECORD_AUDIO（麦克风/录音）
    用途：仅用于「钢琴拾音识谱」功能：通过设备麦克风实时监听真实钢琴（包括原声钢琴和数码钢琴）的声音，识别您演奏的音符，帮助您与乐谱进行对比练习。音频处理在您的设备本地进行，不会录制、存储或上传到任何服务器。
  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT / BLUETOOTH_SCAN / ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION（Android 11 及以下）（蓝牙）
    用途：用于扫描和连接外部蓝牙 MIDI 设备（如蓝牙 MIDI 键盘或控制器），通过低功耗蓝牙（BLE）实现 MIDI 连接。ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION 仅在 Android 11 及以下版本中被申请，这是因为该系统版本要求进行 BLE 扫描必须获得此权限；本应用不会将位置权限用于任何其他目的。在 Android 12 及以上版本，我们使用 neverForLocation 标志申请蓝牙权限，不访问您的位置信息。

上述权限仅用于此处描述的功能目的。您可随时在设备设置中撤销相关权限；撤销权限将导致对应功能无法使用。

任何通过互联网的传输或电子存储方式均无法保证绝对安全。我们会尽力采用合理措施，但无法对安全性做出绝对保证。

我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的可识别个人身份信息。若我们获知在未事先知情的情况下收集到此类信息，将在合理期限内尽快删除。如您是儿童的父母或法定监护人且认为您的孩子向我们提供了此类信息，请与我们联系。

  • 本地数据：您可清除应用数据或卸载本应用；所有本地进度、记录与设置将丢失，我们无法恢复。
  • 卸载：卸载本应用将停止本应用对数据的进一步收集；第三方 SDK 可能已按各自政策收集了数据。

第三方服务收集的信息可能会被传输至您所在国家/地区以外的服务器并在此处理。使用本应用即表示您同意该等传输与处理，并知悉其可能适用不同的数据保护法律。
