Privacy Policy (English)

1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Piano Sight Reading" (the "App"), provided by Lumen Labs ("we", "our", or "us"). It explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App as an overseas user.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account and we do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number directly through the App.

However, certain information may be collected automatically through third-party analytics and advertising services integrated into the App. This information may include:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system version, language settings);
  • Usage data (such as screens you view, features you use, and session duration);
  • Approximate location information (for example, coarse location inferred from IP address);
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or similar identifiers);
  • Crash logs and performance data.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for purposes such as:

  • Improving the stability, performance, and features of the App;
  • Understanding how users interact with the App and optimizing the training and practice experience;
  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services;
  • Showing more relevant advertisements through third-party advertising partners;
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues and potential misuse.

4. Third-Party Services and SDKs

We may use third-party services to provide analytics and advertising within the App. These services may collect and process information as described in their own privacy policies. Such services may include, but are not limited to:

  • Third-party analytics services (for example, Firebase / Google Analytics);
  • Third-party advertising networks (for example, Google AdMob).

The data collected by these third parties may include device information, usage data, advertising identifiers, and coarse location information. We do not have direct control over how these third parties use the information they collect, and their use is governed by their respective privacy policies.

We recommend that you review the privacy policies of these providers for more details on their data practices.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data collected through the App may be processed and stored on the servers of our third-party service providers. We take reasonable measures to protect information processed through the App and to choose service providers that apply appropriate security measures.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is primarily intended for general users and music learners and is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13. Nevertheless, the content of the App is educational and does not contain inappropriate material, and children under 13 may use the App with the knowledge and consent of their parent or legal guardian.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have unintentionally collected personally identifiable information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as reasonably possible. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with such information, please contact us.

7. Your Choices

You may have certain choices in relation to the information we collect:

  • Advertising settings: You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your device settings. These options vary by platform and device.
  • Analytics and usage data: Some platforms or third-party services may provide opt-out options for analytics tracking. Please refer to the privacy settings available on your device or the relevant third-party service.
  • Uninstalling the App: You can stop all information collection by uninstalling the App from your device.

8. International Use

Information collected through the App may be transferred to and processed in countries or regions other than your own. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of information to such countries or regions, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes take effect will constitute your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
