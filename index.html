关于光点工作室

About Luminous Studio

光点工作室致力于用小而美的工具，陪伴音乐学习者与创作者。我们关注真实练习场景，从节奏、视奏，到练习统计和效率管理，希望用技术为每一次努力加一束光。

我们从音乐领域出发，同时也关注更广泛的工具类 App 使用场景，期待用简单专注的设计，解决练习和生活中的具体问题。

Luminous Studio focuses on crafting small yet powerful tools for music learners and creators. From rhythm and sight-reading to practice tracking and productivity, we aim to add a beam of light to every moment of effort.
Starting from music, we also explore broader utility app scenarios, using focused design to solve real problems in both practice and everyday life.

首款应用：钢琴视奏练习

Featured App: Piano Sight-Reading

「钢琴视奏练习」是一款为钢琴学习者打造的日常视奏训练工具，提供随机谱例、难度分级与练习统计，让你在碎片时间也能高效提升视奏能力。

无论你是自学入门，还是正在备考音乐等级考试，都可以用它进行短时、高频、可量化的训练，逐步建立稳定的视奏手感。

“Piano Sight-Reading” is a daily training companion for pianists, offering random scores, difficulty levels, and practice stats so you can boost your sight-reading skills even in short breaks.
Whether you're self-learning or preparing for graded exams, it supports short, frequent, and measurable practice sessions to help you build a reliable sight-reading feel.
随机谱例 · Random Scores
New challenge every time
每次练习都会生成不同的练习谱例，避免“背熟谱子”带来的假进步，让视奏始终保持新鲜感。
Each session brings different score snippets, preventing “memorized pieces” from faking progress and keeping your sight-reading practice fresh.
难度分级 · Level-Based
From beginner to advanced
从入门到进阶逐级扩展音域与节奏型，你可以按自己的状态选择合适的难度，循序渐进提升视奏能力。
Difficulty levels gradually expand ranges and rhythm patterns, so you can choose the level that fits your current stage and improve step by step.
节拍与时限 · Tempo & Time
Practice under real pressure
支持自定义练习速度与视奏时限，模拟真实演奏情境，训练在时间压力下快速理解与落键的能力。
Customize tempo and sight-reading time limits to simulate real-playing conditions and train quick reading under time pressure.
练习统计 · Practice Stats
Progress you can see
记录每天的视奏次数、时长与难度分布，让你的投入与进步都可以被看见，帮助维持长期练习动力。
Track daily sessions, time spent, and difficulty distribution so your effort and progress are visible, helping you stay motivated for long-term practice.
极简界面 · Minimal UI
Focus on the score
打开即练，无复杂操作和多余元素，把注意力还给谱面与双手，让每一分钟都落在真正的练习上。
Launch and practice instantly with a distraction-free interface, so all your time goes into real playing, not navigation.
每日提醒 · Daily Reminders
Build steady habits
通过温和的练习提醒，帮助你养成稳定的视奏训练节奏，让进步成为日常的一部分。
Gentle reminders help you maintain a steady sight-reading routine and turn progress into part of your everyday life.
